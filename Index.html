Yuta Okkotsu

About Yuta

Yuta Okkotsu is a special-grade sorcerer and second year student at Jujutsu Metropolitan High School, who aimed to unravel the curse of his deaceased friend Rika during his first year and gain control over his abilities.

Yuta and Rika

Yuta's Characteristics

  1. He has a near infinte amount of cursed energy
  2. He has the ability to use reversed cusre technique
  3. While he is physically weak, he has immense control over his cursed energy enabling him to strengthen himself to absurd levels

Abilities

  1. Bottomless/infinte cursed energy
  2. Enhanced strength and Speed
  3. Reversed cursed technique
  4. Cursed Technique: Mimicry
  5. Domain Expansion
  6. Innate Technique: Rika
